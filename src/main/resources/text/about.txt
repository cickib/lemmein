<h1 style="text-align:center;">BudapestFlatFinder project</h1>
<h3 style="text-align:center;">The project is <i>heavily</i> in progress.</h3>
<hr>
<p>
This application collects flats from different rental websites based on your requirements.
<br>
Displaying every flat in one integrated table that can be sorted and filtered by every column makes it easier to find your new home.
</p>
<br>
<strong>Tech stack:</strong>
<ul>
<li>Java8/SpringBoot back-end</li>
<li>PostgreSQL db + Hibernate</li>
<li>Angular 1 front-end + traces of Thymeleaf & JQuery</li>
</ul>
